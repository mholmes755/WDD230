Local Spelling B Champion

The town Spelling B took place this past weekend on May 10th - May 12th. People of all age groups participated in thier own divisions. After 2 days we had champions from each of our different age divisions compete in a competitive "spell off" where each individual was asked to spell the same word and everyone who got it right advanced. William Grey, our "champion of champions", won on Sunday by spelling the word "fuchsia" which is both a color and a plant.
